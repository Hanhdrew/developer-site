About me

After serving in the Marine Corps, I sought new challenges that would would push me beyond my comfort zone. With no prior experience in coding, I took a leap of faith and enrolled in a coding bootcamp. There, I immersed myself in Frontend Web Development, and my passion for learning propelled me to become a Fullstack Web Developer. Today, I leverage the discipline and resilience during my military service to build robust, user-centric web applications and continue growing not only as a Web Developer, but to push the boundaries of new fulfilling experiences.
